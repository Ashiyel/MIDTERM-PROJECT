1. PERIODIC TABLE

The Periodic Table of Elements is a chart that organizes all known chemical elements by their atomic number, properties, and electron configuration. It helps scientists understand how elements behave and relate to each other in chemical reactions.

2. SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

This website provides information about Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6): Clean Water and Sanitation. It highlights the importance of ensuring the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all by 2030. The page outlines key targets such as universal access to safe drinking water, improved sanitation and hygiene, reduction of pollution, efficient water use, and the protection of water-related ecosystems. It also offers links to explore other Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

3. SDG CONTACT FORM

This website features a user-friendly form that collects basic personal information—such as name, email, address, age, and gender—and allows users to select the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) they support. The form aims to raise awareness and gather insights on public interest in the 17 global goals set by the United Nations, including goals like No Poverty, Quality Education, Climate Action, and more. Once completed, users can submit their responses with a single click.
